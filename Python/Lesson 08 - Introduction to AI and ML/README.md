## üß¨ Data Science and AI: How it Fits Together

Data Science is an interdisciplinary field that extracts knowledge and insights from data using scientific methods, algorithms, and programming to inform decision-making.


### The Data Science Lifecycle

The process follows a structured, iterative approach:

1. **Problem Definition:** Understanding business/mission needs.
2. **Data Collection & Cleaning:** Preparing raw data for analysis.
3. **Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA):** Finding initial patterns and insights.
4. **Feature Engineering:** Selecting and transforming variables for models.
5. **Model Building & Evaluation:** Training algorithms and testing performance.
6. **Deployment & Monitoring:** Running models in production and maintaining them.

## ‚öñÔ∏è AI Ethics and Responsible Innovation

As AI systems become more integrated into society, understanding the ethical risks is paramount.

### Key Ethical Pillars

- **Bias:** Addressing systematic favoritism that leads to unfair results for certain groups.
- **Explainability:** Being transparent about how models make specific decisions.
- **Privacy:** The responsibility of protecting sensitive personal data.
- **Accountability:** Realizing and taking responsibility for the impact of ML models.
- **Misuse:** Understanding how AI technologies can be weaponized or used for harm.
