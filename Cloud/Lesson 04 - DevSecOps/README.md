### Toyota Production System (TPS)

A philosophy centered on eliminating waste to achieve the most efficient production methods while making work easier for employees.

- **Jidoka:** "Automation with a human touch".
- **Just-In-Time:** Producing only what is needed, when it is needed.


### Lean Management

Focused on maximizing customer value while minimizing waste.

- **Define Value:** Focus strictly on what solves the problem.
- **Map Value Streams:** Utilize tools like Kanban boards to make work visible.
- **Establish Flow:** Keep work batches small to prevent bottlenecks.


### Agile Methodology

A software development approach focused on rapid value delivery through iterative progress.

- **Key Values:** Prioritizing individuals, working software, customer collaboration, and responsiveness to change over rigid processes or plans.


## üîÑ The Three Ways of DevSecOps

The application of manufacturing and leadership principles to the software development value stream is categorized into "Three Ways":

1. **The First Way (Flow):** Keep work batches small and visible to increase speed and innovation. This includes **Continuous Build, Test, Integration, Delivery, and Deployment**.
2. **The Second Way (Feedback):** Shorten the loop between development and deployment to accelerate problem detection and recovery. This involves **Continuous Monitoring** and baking observability into applications.
3. **The Third Way (Continuous Learning):** Foster a high-trust culture that encourages experimentation and learning from failure. This is achieved through **Continuous Improvement** and sprint retrospectives.
